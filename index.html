I only sell seeds with select genetics.

These are not your regular seed packets, and are not intended for the contemporaneous heirloom-style gardeners.

If you expect my seeds to be uniform or predictable, you will be dissapointed, and I will not give you back your money.

These seeds are intended for landrace gardeners and those who are looking to add genetic diversity to their landraces, or start a landrace of their own.

All of my seeds are highly variable and have been locally adapted to Airdrie Alberta, in zone 3. I have high clay, low fertility, alicaceous soil. I select for drought resistance more than disease resistance because we have very few diseases in my highly sterile environs. I also rarely experience any pest pressure to speak of. I don't even have squirrels.

Ground Rules

I'll ship seeds anywhere in the world any time of the year.

If you're far away from Canada don't expect mail any time soon.

All seeds have their own purchase link which makes buying lots of seeds less convenient, so take it easy, alright?.
